Infinity Stone Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four of the 6 infinity stones to help power up the gaulet. By clicking on a stones you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if you over power the gaunlet

The value of each infinity stone is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time the game restarts, the value of each infinity stone will change
